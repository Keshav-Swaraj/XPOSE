# XPOSE – Your Personal Financial Guardian

**XPOSE** is a free browser extension + web platform that protects everyday Indians from financial manipulation and confusion on the internet.

## Tagline
XPOSE decodes, shields & exposes

## Core Problem It Solves

Indian financial websites frequently use dark patterns (hidden fees, pre-ticked boxes, fake urgency, confusing jargon) that cause the average user to lose thousands of rupees every year. At the same time, complex loan agreements, insurance policies, and investment pages are hard to understand, especially in regional languages. This affects crores of middle-class families, gig workers, and first-time users.

## How XPOSE Works

### One-Time Install
Install the lightweight Chrome extension. No signup, no personal data collected (zero-trust model).

### Real-Time Protection on Financial Websites
- Automatically scans loan, insurance, credit card, investment, and checkout pages.
- Detects dark patterns (pre-ticked insurance, hidden fees, fake timers, guilt-tripping language) and shows a clear red warning banner with exact money impact.

### Smart Explain Mode (The Killer Feature)
- Click the XPOSE icon or “Explain Page” button.
- AI reads the entire page and gives a simple summary in your chosen Indian language (Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, etc.).
- Highlights important sections directly on the webpage: risks, fees, rules, cancellation policy (color-coded: Green = Safe, Yellow = Attention, Red = Risky).
- Conversational Q&A: Ask follow-up questions like “What happens if I miss an EMI?” or “Is this insurance worth it?” — XPOSE answers based on the fine print.

### Voice Mode
- Tap “Listen” to hear the summary or explanations spoken aloud in your selected language.

### One-Tap Regulatory Action
- Found something suspicious? Tap “Report to SEBI” — XPOSE auto-fills a complaint with screenshot, page details, and violated regulation (mock integration with SCORES for demo; real API in future).

### XPOSE Dashboard (xpose.in)
- Click the extension button $\rightarrow$ “Open Dashboard”.
- Live Leaderboard with Pressure Scores and trajectory (which sites are getting better or worse).
- Personal savings tracker (“XPOSE saved you ₹X this month”).
- Shareable XPOSE Score pages (e.g., `xpose.in/score/policybazaar.com`).
- Transparency reports and educational guides.

## Additional Helpful Features

- **Reality Check Calculator**: Shows true cost of loans/EMIs including hidden charges.
- **Insurance Scanner**: Upload policy PDF $\rightarrow$ get simple explanation + key exclusions.
- **Family Sharing**: Share read-only view with family members.
- **Graceful on Non-Financial Sites**: Stays silent by default or offers general summarization if enabled.

## Business & Impact Model
