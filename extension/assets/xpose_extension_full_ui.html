Consent data harvesting
Critical
A hidden or pre-ticked checkbox collects your consent to share personal and vehicle data with unnamed third parties — without clearly identifying who they are or how the data will be used.
IRDAI 2016
Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority requires explicit, informed consent before sharing policyholder data with any third party. Pre-ticked boxes do not qualify as informed consent.
Deceptive price anchoring
Moderate
"Lowest Price Guaranteed" banner displayed before IDV is set, anchoring the user to an incomplete price. The true total cost including GST, add-ons, and IDV correction is not shown upfront.
CPA 2019
Consumer Protection Act Section 2(47) defines unfair trade practice to include misleading price representations. Displaying an incomplete price as "guaranteed lowest" without disclosing total cost qualifies.
